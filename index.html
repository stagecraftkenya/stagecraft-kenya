What is StageCraft Kenya?

StageCraft Kenya is a digital creative platform that designs child-centered modern dance scripts aligned with the Kenya National Drama & Film Festival themes.

WHY: To help schools compete confidently with scripts that are theme-accurate, time-controlled (7 minutes), and adjudicator-friendly.

HOW: Through carefully structured concepts that include theme interpretation, clear settings, character flow, and stage milestones.

WHERE: Designed for preliminaries, county, regional, and national festival stages.
